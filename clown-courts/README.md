# Clown Courts 🤡⚖️🌈

**Clown Courts** is a JOY-side humor chamber for silly verdicts, goofy logic trials, playful meme cases, and safe family laughter.

It connects to Meme Court and Goblin Court as a softer, brighter space.

## Purpose

Clown Courts helps people laugh without losing kindness.

It is for:

- silly arguments
- harmless jokes
- goofy captions
- playful verdicts
- confidence building
- learning how to be funny safely
- remembering that joy is allowed

## Safety Rules

1. No private information.
2. No full names, school names, phone numbers, birthdays, or exact locations.
3. No bullying.
4. No jokes about someone’s body, disability, grief, trauma, or identity.
5. No pretending memes are facts.
6. No fake court authority.
7. If the joke would hurt someone, rewrite it softer.
