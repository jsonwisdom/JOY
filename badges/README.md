## What This Gallery Does

- Gives newcomers an instant emotional map of JOYSPACE.
- Shows the visual grammar: circles, sparkles, pastels, and hybrid highlights.
- Makes the badge system feel real, inviting, and earnable.
- Anchors the quest arc in a single glance.
- Serves as the visual index for future expansions, seasonal badges, and lineage badges.

## Boundary

This gallery is an invitation layer.

It is not a scoring system for human worth.

Badges recognize participation, care, safety, creativity, and growth.

Authority remains false.
