## Purpose

The Badge Picker helps a new Joy Gardener choose a first badge in under two minutes.

It is simple, warm, beginner-safe, and pressure-free.

## Start Here

Pick the line that feels most like you today.

```text
I want to build my first garden -> Seed Planter 🪴
I want to stay safer online -> Future Protector 🛡️
I want to show growth -> Growth Guardian 🌱✨
I want to brighten someone's day -> Joy Bringer 🌈
I want to practice care -> Kindness Keeper 💛
```

## Badge Choices

### Seed Planter 🪴

Choose this if you want to create your first Digital Garden.

Tiny first action:

```text
Write three lines about who you are, what you like, and what you want to learn.
```

### Future Protector 🛡️

Choose this if you want to learn online safety.

Tiny first action:

```text
Review one privacy setting or write one safety rule for yourself.
```

### Growth Guardian 🌱✨

Choose this if you want to reflect on progress.

Tiny first action:

```text
Write one sentence about something you learned recently.
```

### Joy Bringer 🌈

Choose this if you want to make someone's day brighter.

Tiny first action:

```text
Send or create one kind, funny, or encouraging thing.
```

### Kindness Keeper 💛

Choose this if you want to practice care.

Tiny first action:

```text
Notice one way you helped, supported, or listened to someone.
```

## Proof Reminder

Proof can be small.

Proof can be private.

Proof can be:

- a note
- a drawing
- a screenshot
- a commit
- a reflection
- a trusted witness

Proof shows that something happened.

Proof does not measure your worth.

## Privacy Reminder

You may keep your proof:

```text
private
family-only
shareable
```

Private proof counts.

No one has to post publicly to belong in JOYSPACE.

## If You Are Unsure

Choose Seed Planter.

A garden can start with one sentence.
