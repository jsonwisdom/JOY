The Lineage Diagram Spec transforms machine-readable badge lineage into a human-readable map.

It helps newcomers understand how the five JOYSPACE badges relate without turning them into levels, ranks, or authority.

## Pillars

JOYSPACE has two structural pillars.

### Self-Creation Path

```text
Seed Planter -> Growth Guardian -> Joy Bringer
```

Meaning:

```text
Identity begins.
Identity deepens.
Identity radiates.
```

### Safety & Care Path

```text
Future Protector -> Kindness Keeper
```

Meaning:

```text
Safety is sovereignty.
Safety becomes care.
```

## Cross-Arc Principle

```text
A safe self can safely shine.
```

## Cross-Arc Guards

- Without Future Protector, Joy Bringer can become risky.
- Without Growth Guardian, Kindness Keeper can become self-erasing.
- Without Seed Planter, none of it has a root.
- Without Kindness Keeper, the system lacks warmth.

## Visual Direction

A future visual diagram should feel:

- welcoming
- soft
- bright
- beginner-safe
- non-competitive
- garden-like
- readable at a glance

Suggested visual motifs:

- two gentle arcs
- soft pastel lines
- no podiums
- no ranking numbers
- no trophies as superiority symbols
- circular or garden-path layout preferred

## Diagram Rules

The visual diagram must not imply:

- hierarchy of human worth
- ranking
- scoring
- superiority
- permanent status
- institutional authority

The visual diagram may imply:

- growth
- safety
- care
- creativity
- reflection
- invitation
