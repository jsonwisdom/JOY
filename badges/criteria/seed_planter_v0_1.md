# Seed Planter Criteria V0.1

Status: ACTIVE
Authority: false
Badge: seed_planter
Arc: self_creation_path

## What This Badge Invites

Creating your first Digital Garden: a README that shows who you are, what you care about, and what you are growing toward.

## What Counts as Evidence

Any of the following:

- A GitHub repository named after your username with a README.md
- A screenshot of the README
- A short reflection describing what you wrote and why
- A drawing or mind-map of your Digital Garden
- A trusted witness confirming the README exists

## Proof Model

Accepted proof types:

- reflection
- screenshot
- drawing
- commit
- trusted_witness
- journal_entry

Proof confirms existence, not quality.

## Witness Model

A witness may be:

- self
- friend
- mentor
- family
- teacher
- guide
- community

Witnesses confirm existence, not quality.
