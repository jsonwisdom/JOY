# Growth Guardian Criteria V0.1

Status: ACTIVE
Authority: false
Badge: growth_guardian
Arc: self_creation_path

## What This Badge Invites

Showing ongoing self-development, reflection, or improvement.

## What Counts as Evidence

Any of the following:

- A short reflection on something you learned
- A commit showing progress on a project
- A journal entry
- A drawing or diagram of your growth
- A witness confirming consistent effort

## Proof Model

Accepted proof types:

- reflection
- drawing
- commit
- trusted_witness
- journal_entry
- photo

Proof confirms continuity, not achievement level.

## Witness Model

A witness may be:

- self
- friend
- mentor
- family
- teacher
- guide
- community

Witnesses confirm continuity, not quality.

## Award Record

Award fires when:

```text
badge_id = growth_guardian
proof_exists = true
authority = false
```

## Lineage Alignment

Growth Guardian is the middle of the Self-Creation Path.

It bridges:

```text
Seed Planter -> Growth Guardian -> Joy Bringer
```

## Safety Notes

- Reflections may remain private.
- No public posting is required.
- Private proof counts.
- Authority remains false.
