# Joy Bringer Criteria V0.1

Status: ACTIVE
Authority: false
Badge: joy_bringer
Arc: self_creation_path

## What This Badge Invites

Bringing delight, creativity, or encouragement to someone's day.

## What Counts as Evidence

Any of the following:

- A message you wrote that encouraged someone
- A creative act you shared, such as a drawing, code, poem, or idea
- A reflection on how you brightened someone's day
- A trusted witness confirming you brought joy

## Proof Model

Accepted proof types:

- reflection
- screenshot
- drawing
- commit
- trusted_witness
- photo
- journal_entry

Proof confirms the act existed, not its scale.

## Witness Model

A witness may be:

- self
- friend
- mentor
- family
- teacher
- guide
- community

Witnesses confirm impact or participation, not quality.

## Award Record

Award fires when:

```text
badge_id = joy_bringer
proof_exists = true
authority = false
```

## Lineage Alignment

Joy Bringer is the radiant end of the Self-Creation Path.

It completes:

```text
Seed Planter -> Growth Guardian -> Joy Bringer
```

## Safety Notes

- No requirement to share private messages.
- Do not expose another person's private information.
- Private proof counts.
- Authority remains false.
