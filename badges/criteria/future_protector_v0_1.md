## What This Badge Invites

Learning and applying online safety practices that protect your future self.

## What Counts as Evidence

Any of the following:

- A completed safety reflection document
- A checklist of safety settings you reviewed
- A screenshot of updated privacy settings without sensitive details
- A written note about what you learned
- A trusted witness confirming the reflection was completed

## Proof Model

Accepted proof types:

- reflection
- screenshot
- drawing
- commit
- trusted_witness
- journal_entry

Proof confirms engagement, not correctness.

## Witness Model

A witness may be:

- self
- friend
- mentor
- family
- teacher
- guide
- community

Witnesses confirm engagement, not quality.
