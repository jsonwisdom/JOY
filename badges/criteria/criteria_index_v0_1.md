## Purpose

The Criteria Layer defines how badges are earned.

Criteria are invitations, not tests.

Evidence is flexible, human, and self-chosen.

## Core Principles

- Badges measure actions, not worth.
- Evidence can be a note, reflection, screenshot, commit, drawing, or trusted witness.
- No badge requires perfection.
- No badge creates authority.
- All criteria must align with lineage metadata.
- All criteria must be achievable by beginners.
- All criteria must be safe for teenagers.
