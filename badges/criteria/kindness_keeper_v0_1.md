# Kindness Keeper Criteria V0.1

Status: ACTIVE
Authority: false
Badge: kindness_keeper
Arc: safety_care_path

## What This Badge Invites

Practicing empathy, care, or support within your community.

## What Counts as Evidence

Any of the following:

- A note describing an act of kindness
- A reflection on how you helped someone
- A screenshot of a supportive message with privacy respected
- A trusted witness confirming the act

## Proof Model

Accepted proof types:

- reflection
- screenshot
- drawing
- commit
- trusted_witness
- photo
- journal_entry

Proof confirms care existed, not perfection.

## Witness Model

A witness may be:

- self
- friend
- mentor
- family
- teacher
- guide
- community

Witnesses confirm care, not quality.

- No requirement to disclose sensitive situations.
- Do not expose another person's private information.
- Private proof counts.
