# JOYSPACE Witness Model V0.1

Status: ACTIVE
Authority: false
Family Barrier: INTACT
Layer: Human Confirmation
Depends On: JOYSPACE Proof Model V0.1

## Purpose

The Witness Model defines how people may confirm evidence in JOYSPACE without creating authority, judgment, rank, or pressure.

Witnesses confirm existence.

Witnesses do not judge quality.

Witnesses do not measure worth.

## Doctrine

- Badges invite.
- Evidence exists.
- Witnesses confirm existence.
- Witnesses do not grade.
- Witnesses do not rank.
- Witnesses do not create authority.
- Celebration follows confirmation.
- Authority remains false.

## Who May Be a Witness

A witness may be:

- the badge earner
- a friend
- a mentor
- a family member
- a teacher
- a JOYSPACE Guide
- a trusted community member

Self-witness is valid when the proof is a reflection, drawing, journal entry, private screenshot, or personal learning note.

## What Witnesses Confirm

Witnesses may confirm:

- the proof exists
- the badge earner participated
- the evidence matches the badge invitation
- privacy and safety boundaries were respected

Witnesses may not confirm:

- that a person is better than another person
- that the proof is high quality
- that the badge earner has permanent status
- that the witness has authority over the earner

## Safety Rules

- A witness must not pressure someone to make private proof public.
- A witness must not request passwords, private messages, private locations, school details, or sensitive screenshots.
- A witness must not override the badge earner's privacy choice.
- A witness may decline without shame.
- A badge earner may choose a different witness.

## Witness Lifecycle

### 1. Proof Created

The badge earner creates or identifies evidence.

### 2. Witness Invited

A witness may be invited when useful or required by the criteria.

### 3. Existence Confirmed

The witness confirms the proof exists and matches the badge invitation.

### 4. Privacy Preserved

The proof remains private, family-only, or shareable according to the earner's choice.

### 5. Celebration

The badge may be celebrated without ranking, grading, or authority.

## Alignment With Criteria

Criteria files may request witness confirmation, but must explain that witnesses confirm existence only.

No criteria file may require a witness to judge quality.

No criteria file may require public disclosure of private evidence.
