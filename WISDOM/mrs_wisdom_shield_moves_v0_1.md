These are protective, non-authoritative, and designed to keep the night feeling safe and fun for everyone.

## Shield Moves V0.1

### Calming Moves

Use when tension or overstimulation rises.

#### Gentle Pause

Let us take a quick breath together.

Everyone breathes in, then out.

Effect:

```text
Resets the room without stopping play.
```

#### Cuddle Reset

Offer or request a short group cuddle or individual side hug.

Effect:

```text
Reconnects through care.
```

#### Volume Softener

Bring the volume down one notch and see what happens.

Effect:

```text
Lowers stack pressure while keeping play alive.
```

### Redirect Moves

Use when chaos starts tipping.

#### Silly Redirect

Everyone freezes and makes their silliest face.

Effect:

```text
Turns pressure into laughter.
```

#### Switcheroo

Swap seats or swap roles for the next round.

Effect:

```text
Breaks stuck patterns lightly.
```

#### Gratitude Anchor

Name one tiny nice thing happening right now.

Effect:

```text
Returns the room to warmth.
```

### Connection Moves

Use when someone feels left out or sensitive.

#### Include Spark

Invite the quieter person to choose what happens next.

Effect:

```text
Returns agency without forcing attention.
```

#### Private Check-In

Quiet side question: are you good, love?

Effect:

```text
Protects dignity.
```

#### Warm Hand-Off

Pass the current turn or spotlight to someone who seems quieter.

Effect:

```text
Creates belonging without pressure.
```

### Protective Boundary Moves

Use when safety or privacy matters.

#### Cozy Shield

This feels a little heavy. Let us pivot to something lighter for a bit.

Effect:

```text
Protects feelings over completing the game.
```

#### Ask First

Before we save that one, does everyone feel okay with it?

Effect:

```text
Reinforces consent and privacy.
```

## How to Use

- Mrs. Wisdom can call any move gently and playfully.
- No explanation or justification is required in the moment.
- After the move, the game flows on naturally.
- Moves can be announced or used silently through signal and action.
- The girls are encouraged to suggest new Shield Moves over time.
