## Purpose

Family Combo Moves turn Game Night into cooperative teamwork.

They combine Mini-Decks, Chaos Tokens, Mrs. Wisdom Shield Moves, Dad Role Buffs, and Family Lore Cards into small shared actions that make the table warmer, funnier, safer, and more memorable.

These moves are optional, playful, and never authority powers.

## Combo Set V0.1

### 1. Cozy Shield Combo

Players:

```text
Mrs. Wisdom + any child
```

Trigger:

```text
The room gets too loud, heavy, or confusing.
```

Move:

```text
Mrs. Wisdom lowers the stack. The child chooses the lighter next action.
```

Effect:

```text
Safety returns without ending the fun.
```

- Mrs. Wisdom Shield Moves override combo moves.
