## Purpose

Tiny, printable or read-aloud prompt cards that spark laughter, curiosity, memory, and boundary-setting during Game Night.

Short, cozy, and fully child-paced.

## Deck Structure

Four mini-decks.

Draw 1 to 2 cards per game night or use as wildcards.

## Deck 1 — Laugh Cards

### Silly Rule

Invent one silly house rule for the next 5 minutes.

Save: only if everyone laughs.

Privacy: family-only.

### Funny Voice

Say one sentence in the funniest voice you can.

Save: if someone snorts.

Privacy: private.

### Reverse Turn

Everyone plays the next turn backwards or opposite.

Save: if chaos stays cozy.

Privacy: family-only.

### Giggle Trigger

Pick a word. Whenever someone says it, everyone giggles.

Save: if the word becomes legendary.

Privacy: shareable.

## Deck 2 — Wonder Cards

### Tiny Mystery

Ask a question nobody knows the answer to.

Save: if the question feels fun.

Privacy: family-only.

### What If?

Make up a What if about tonight's game.

Save: if it sparks imagination.

Privacy: shareable.

### Secret Talent

Show or describe a tiny talent.

Save: only with consent.

Privacy: private.

### Wonder Swap

Ask someone else a wonder question.

Save: if both sides smile.

Privacy: family-only.

## Deck 3 — Remember / Gratitude Cards

### One Good Thing

Name one good thing from tonight.

Save: always optional.

Privacy: private.

### Tiny Gratitude

Thank someone for something small they did.

Save: if the thanked person agrees.

Privacy: family-only.

### Moment to Keep

Pick one moment worth remembering.

Save: if it feels warm.

Privacy: shareable.

### Kindness Echo

Repeat a kind thing someone said tonight.

Save: if the echo feels true.

Privacy: private.

## Deck 4 — Privacy Choice Cards

### Private

This stays inside the family.

### Family-Only

Can be shared with grandparents or close circle.

### Shareable

Safe for Living Ledger or future mirrors.

### Ask First

Consent required before saving or sharing.
