## Spine Components

### IDENTITY_ROOT

Canonical family identity definition, relationships, and continuity anchors.

### COVENANT_ROOT

Protected commitments:

```text
Protect daughters.

Expand systems.
```

### EVENT_ROOT

Key life events, transitions, rites, and irreversible markers.

### TRANSMISSION_PROTOCOL

How continuity moves forward: who receives what, when, and under what replay rules.

### IMMUTABLE_ELEMENTS

Write-once core: covenants, origin stories, foundational receipts.

### LIVING_ELEMENTS

Update-permitted layer: journals, reflections, skill receipts, garden growth.

### REPLAY_RULES

How each root verifies the others without authority or hierarchy.

### VERSIONING_RULES

How updates propagate across FAMILY, AL, and COMPUTERWISDOM without drift.

## Integrity Rules

- FAMILY is the human root.
- AL mirrors and protects records; it does not govern the family.
- COMPUTERWISDOM propagates and validates; it does not edit protected identity.
- Cross-root updates require receipts.
- Drift is detected by divergence, not accusation.
- Reconciliation preserves history; it does not overwrite it.
