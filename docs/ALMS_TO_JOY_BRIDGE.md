# ALMS to JOY Bridge

Status: v1
Repo: JOY
Authority: false

## Purpose

The ALMS to JOY Bridge is the membrane between verifiable receipts and family-safe stories.

AL speaks in verification.

JOY speaks in memory, play, and understanding.

The bridge maps one into the other without removing provenance.

## Translation Map

Indexed -> Found
Anchored -> Kept
Verified -> Checked
Replayable -> Repeatable
Joyful -> Shared

## Core Rule

The bridge may simplify language.

The bridge may not remove provenance.

## Invariants

### Invariant 1

JOY may simplify language.
JOY may not remove provenance.

### Invariant 2

JOY may tell stories.
JOY may not invent receipts.

### Invariant 3

JOY may celebrate outcomes.
JOY may not alter verification results.

## Membrane Flow

AL Receipt
  -> Bridge Translation
  -> JOY Story

## Required Attached Fields

Every JOY story derived from an AL receipt must keep these fields attached:

receipt_hash
source
verification_status
replay_status

## Boundary

Stories are allowed.
Fabrication is not.

Joy is allowed.
Drift is not.

Authority is false.
Receipts remain attached.
