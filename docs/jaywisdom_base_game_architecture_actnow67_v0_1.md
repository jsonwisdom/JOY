### Employee Layer

Employees of clown courts and beyond are never converted into targets by default.

They are categorized only by documented role, public record, or official source.

Forbidden:

- private doxxing
- guessing intent
- guilt by employment
- financial accusation without adjudicated or official source
- converting payroll or transfer visibility into misconduct

Allowed:

- public salary records where lawful
- official audit findings
- court records
- inspector general reports
- procurement records
- conflict-of-interest disclosures
- settlement records
- official banking/transfer findings when lawfully published
