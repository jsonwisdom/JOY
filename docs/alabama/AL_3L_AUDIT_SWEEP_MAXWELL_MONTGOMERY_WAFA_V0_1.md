## OODA Loop

### Observe

Collect public surfaces around:

- Maxwell Air Force Base / Gunter Annex public information
- Montgomery city and county interfaces
- Montgomery Public Schools public records where relevant
- DoDEA or on-base school public interfaces where relevant
- Public procurement notices
- Public meeting agendas and minutes
- Public safety, transportation, and infrastructure connections
- Local reporting and public statements

### Orient

Sort surfaces into lanes:

```text
Records Lane
Contracts Lane
Schools Lane
Veterans and Military Families Lane
Public Safety Lane
Infrastructure Lane
Media Narrative Lane
```

### Decide

Promote only lanes with public records value.

Do not chase vibes. Do not target private people. Do not imply wrongdoing without records.

### Act

Create narrow public-records packets and evidence logs.

### Preserve

Save request text, timestamps, responses, attachments, hashes, and custody notes.

### Replay

Create public-safe summaries only after the evidence chain is preserved.

## Worker Model

