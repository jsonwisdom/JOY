## Game Premise

Players move through everyday life scenarios and answer wisdom prompts.

The goal is not to win by dominating.

The goal is to build trust, practice judgment, and leave everyone safer than before.

## Family Edition Lanes

### 1. Joy Lane

Prompt type:

- What made someone smile today?
- What small thing deserves thanks?
- What memory should be protected?

Rule:

```text
Joy is evidence of life working.
```

### 2. Care Lane

Prompt type:

- Who needs help without being embarrassed?
- What can be fixed quietly?
- What should be handled with patience?

Rule:

```text
Care before correction.
```

### 3. Truth Lane

Prompt type:

- What do we know?
- What are we guessing?
- What receipt would prove it?

Rule:

```text
Truth can be checked without being cruel.
```

### 4. Boundary Lane

Prompt type:

- What should stay private?
- What should not be posted?
- What requires permission first?

Rule:

```text

```

### 5. Repair Lane

Prompt type:

- What went wrong?
- What apology is owed?
- What small action repairs trust?

Rule:

```text
Repair is stronger than pretending nothing happened.
```

## Card Types

### Wisdom Card

A question that helps a player slow down and think.

Example:

```text
Name one thing you can prove, one thing you feel, and one thing you should ask before acting.
```

### Joy Card

A question that protects delight.

Example:

```text
What is one small moment from today that deserves to be remembered?
```

### Boundary Card

A question that protects privacy and consent.

Example:

```text
Would this be okay to share publicly, or should it stay inside the family circle?
```

### Repair Card

A question that moves conflict toward care.

Example:

```text
What is the smallest honest repair that would make this better?
```

### Receipt Card

A question that separates story from evidence.

Example:

```text
What receipt, message, note, photo, calendar entry, or witness would confirm what happened?
```
