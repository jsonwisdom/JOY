## Question Set — China Bridge

### 1. Identity / 身份

```text
Who am I in Chinese?
Who am I in English?
What part of me stays true in both languages?
```

### 2. Family / 家庭

```text
What does my family hope I carry with me?
What should I tell them when I feel far away?
What family rhythm can I keep while abroad?
```

### 3. Language / 语言

```text
What do I need help saying in English?
What do I not want to lose in Chinese?
Where do I need translation, not judgment?
```

### 4. Learning / 学习

```text
What is one class or skill I am ready to learn?
What is one learning habit from China that can still help me?
What is one new Auburn habit I want to try?
```

### 5. Belonging / 归属感

```text
Where do I feel seen?
Where do I feel invisible?
Who can walk beside me this week?
```

### 6. Culture / 文化

```text
What do I want others to understand about where I come from?
What do I want to learn about Alabama and Auburn?
What can I share without feeling exposed?
```

### 7. Safety / 安全

```text
Who do I contact if I feel lost, unsafe, confused, or overwhelmed?
What information should stay private?
What is one safe step I can take today?
```

### 8. Return / 回望与回馈

```text
How do I send the elevator back down?
What would I tell the next student from China?
How do I go far and still come home whole?
```
