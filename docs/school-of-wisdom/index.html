The Engine

Something happened. Something else did not. Humans treated them as equivalent. That substitution is the joke.

Humor is signal, not evidence. Culture is playable, but people are not targets. No receipt, no authority.

Spot a Substitution

Spot a Substitution

Ask three questions:

  1. What happened?
  2. What did not happen?
  3. Did someone treat them as the same thing?

If yes, you found the lesson. Preserve first. Argue later.
