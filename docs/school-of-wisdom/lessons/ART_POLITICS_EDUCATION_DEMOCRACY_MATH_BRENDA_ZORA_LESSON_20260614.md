## 4. Lesson Departments

### 4.1 Art

Art is the expressive layer.

In this lesson, art means:

```text
character
symbol
story
visual frame
public teaching surface
```

Art may inspire, but art does not prove.

Brenda rule:

```text
ART_GREEN requires a file, render, page, or approved artifact.
```

### 4.2 Politics

Politics is the power-and-decision layer.

In this lesson, politics means:

```text
who decides
who benefits
who is represented
what process is visible
what evidence can be challenged
```

Politics must not become personal targeting.

Brenda rule:

```text
POLITICS_GREEN requires public evidence, neutral framing, and no private targeting.
```

### 4.3 Education

Education is the teaching layer.

In this lesson, education means:

```text
explain the rule
show the example
name the boundary
let the learner replay it
```

Brenda rule:

```text
EDUCATION_GREEN requires a lesson artifact and a replay path.
```

### 4.4 Democracy Math

Democracy Math is the verification layer for public decision systems.

In this lesson, democracy math means:

```text
claim + evidence + process + challenge path + receipt = public trust candidate
```

Democracy Math does not say a claim is true because someone powerful says it.

Brenda rule:

```text
DEMOCRACY_MATH_GREEN requires the evidence class and the challenge path.
```
