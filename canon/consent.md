# JOY Consent Doctrine v0.1

## Consent Scope

Consent is required for:

- family artifacts
- public render of personal or relational content
- any use of private data in governed decisions

## Consent States

- **explicit:** documented, replayable consent exists.
- **pending:** consent requested but not yet granted.
- **not_applicable:** artifact does not touch family or personal data.

## Rules

- No public render without explicit consent.
- No fake green: consent must be real, documented, and replayable.
- Mrs. Wisdom review is required for any change that alters consent boundaries.

## Receipts

Every consent decision must produce a receipt:

- subject(s)
- scope
- duration (if applicable)
- decision (granted/denied/revoked)
- replay reference
