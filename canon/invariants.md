# JOY Invariants v0.1

- **JOY_AUTHORITY_FALSE**  
  JOY does not grant or assume authority. All artifacts are non-authoritative evidence.

- **JOY_NO_FAKE_GREEN**  
  No “green” state may be implied without explicit, replayable proof and consent.

- **JOY_CONSENT_REQUIRED_FOR_FAMILY_ARTIFACTS**  
  Any artifact touching family context requires explicit consent before render or merge.

- **JOY_REPLAY_REQUIRED_FOR_PUBLIC_RENDER**  
  Public render is only allowed when replay evidence exists and passes.

- **JOY_RECEIPT_REQUIRED_FOR_GOVERNED_MERGE**  
  Every governed merge must attach at least one receipt documenting the decision.

- **JOY_EXTERNAL_ADAPTERS_ARE_NOT_SOURCES_OF_TRUTH**  
  External systems (chains, platforms, APIs) are adapters only; JOY’s canon and receipts remain the source of truth.
