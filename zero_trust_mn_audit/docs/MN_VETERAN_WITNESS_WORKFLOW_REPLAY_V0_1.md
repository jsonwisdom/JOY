## Core Rule

One witness packet.  
One receipt.  
One replay note.  
No fake green.

## Witness Status

Veterans and citizens may submit voluntary witness statements.

A witness statement is not proof by itself.  
It becomes stronger only when supported by records, receipts, hashes, filings, notices, or agency responses.
