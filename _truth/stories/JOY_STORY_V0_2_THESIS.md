## Thesis

THE_KITCHEN_TABLE_AND_THE_LEDGER tests whether JoySpace can welcome people into the civic-memory system without becoming the truth engine.

The kitchen table represents warmth, conversation, care, disagreement, celebration, and safe entry.

The ledger represents receipts, timestamps, replay, continuity, and verification.

JoySpace may welcome, remember, and celebrate.

JoySpace may not replace receipts.

Stories may create meaning.

The ledger must preserve evidence.

Authority remains false.
