## Purpose

Every person in the Family JoySpace should have a minimum beautiful home structure.

The goal is simple: nobody is just a random file, loose photo, or forgotten page.

Each person gets a clear place to land, remember, share, and grow.

---

## Minimum Family JoySpace Standard

Every Family JoySpace member should have, at minimum:

1. **Homepage**
   - The beautiful front door.
   - This is what opens in Chrome first.
   - Love, identity, and welcome belong here.

2. **Index**
   - The map.
   - Lists the person's pages, receipts, photos, stories, and links.
   - This is the family-friendly table of contents.

3. **Pictures**
   - Photos, artwork, cards, drawings, and visual memories.
   - Public-safe only unless the family explicitly marks a private lane outside the public repo.

4. **Words**
   - Messages, stories, birthday notes, thank-you notes, poems, letters, jokes, and memories.
   - Plain English first.

5. **Links**
   - Buttons to related pages, print cards, JoySpace gardens, Zora gates, GitHub receipts, or public proof trails.
   - Links should serve the person, not bury them in plumbing.

6. **Super Secret Sister Syntax**
   - A playful JOY protocol layer for family-specific rituals, naming, inside jokes, approval phrases, and sister/family routing.
   - This is not for real secrets, passwords, private data, or anything unsafe.
   - Rule: cute names are okay; actual secrets never go in public.

## Routing Rule

Family sees the beautiful page first.

Receipts come second.

```text
PERSON -> HOMEPAGE -> INDEX -> PICTURES / WORDS / LINKS -> RECEIPTS
```

Never route a family member into GitHub plumbing before the JoySpace front door unless they specifically ask for proof.

- private addresses
- private phone numbers
- medical details
