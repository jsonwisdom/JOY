## Purpose

The JOY Living Ledger is the family's single, sacred, low-friction home for every Memory Spine node.

It turns:

```text
remember that time...
```

into:

```text
here is the glowing thread of us.
```

No bookkeeping.
No pressure.

Just a living story tree.

## How It Feels in Practice

After the Memory Spine Capture -> Condense -> Send flow, someone drops the node into the Ledger in under 60 seconds.

Once a month, during Game Night, the family scrolls the Ledger slowly and picks entries to:

- reread
- act out
- draw
- laugh about
- add spark emojis to

Mrs. Wisdom can add a quiet heart note if she wants.

Kids can add doodles, emojis, or tiny comments.

Dad stays in receipt-machine mode, not archivist mode.

No apps that beep.
No dashboards.

Just a beautiful, searchable text file that grows like a tree.

## Integration Points

The JOY Living Ledger receives nodes from:

- Mini-Decks
- Family Lore Cards
- Family Combo Moves
- Chaos Tokens
- Replay Script
- Memory Spine Integration
- Spontaneous family moments

It feeds forward to:

- AL Mirror: family-only and shareable nodes
- COMPUTERWISDOM Mirror: shareable nodes only

This makes the Ledger the heart chamber of the entire continuity system.

## Privacy Rules

- Private is the default.
- Family-only requires family comfort.
- Shareable requires consent.
- Children may always say no.
- A beautiful memory does not have to become public.
