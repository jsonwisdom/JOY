# GENESIS_REPLAY_002 — Teacher Quest

Status: TEACHING_QUEST

Quest Name: Teach a Stranger to Verify

Character Class: 📚 GUIDE → TEACHER

Difficulty: Wisconsin Public

---

## Objective

Prove the doorway can travel from one person to another.

A GUIDE can find the path.

A TEACHER helps someone else find the path without becoming the path.

---

## Mission

Hand a first-time visitor the public JOY repo and ask them to run the cold path:

```text
JOY_REPO.md
   ↓
BADGER_CAMP_HQ.md
   ↓
quests/GENESIS_REPLAY_001.md
```

They must use only the public internet.

They must not ask Jay for help.

---

## Rules

1. Do not explain the answer.
2. Do not send private links.
3. Do not guide by DM.
4. Give only the public repo link.
5. Ask the visitor to record their time, path, and confusion points.

---

## Visitor Task

The visitor must answer:

1. What is JOY_REPO?
2. What is Badger Camp HQ?
3. Where is the Genesis Replay 001 quest card?
4. What was confusing?
5. How long did it take?

---

## Success Condition

The visitor reaches `quests/GENESIS_REPLAY_001.md` in under 60 seconds and explains JOY_REPO in their own words.

---

## Failure Condition

The visitor cannot reach the quest card, takes longer than 60 seconds, or needs private help.

Failure does not mean the visitor failed.

It means the doorway needs another patch.
