## Pattern Reinforcement Gate

Before any future claim is promoted, require:

1. a named public artifact;
2. a timestamped observation surface;
3. a content hash or equivalent byte-identity proof;
4. an independent witness or replay surface;
5. a downgrade path if the artifact does not match the claim.

If the artifact is missing, the claim remains unpromoted.  
If the artifact exists but does not support the claim, the claim moves under audit.  
If replay fails, the pattern is preserved as a new Whammy candidate.
