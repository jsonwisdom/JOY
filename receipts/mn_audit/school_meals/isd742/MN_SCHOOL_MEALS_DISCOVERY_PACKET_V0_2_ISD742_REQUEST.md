Subject: Public Records Request – School Meal Programs, Policies, Fees, and Systems (MN_SCHOOL_MEALS_DISCOVERY_PACKET_V0_2) – ISD 742

Dear ISD 742 Records Custodian / Food Service Director,

This is a public records request under Minnesota law for aggregate, district-level records concerning school meal programs.

Records requested:

1. Current school meal policies, handbooks, and administrative procedures, including meal charge policies, collection procedures, and hardship provisions.
2. Names and contract details of current food service management companies, vendors, or third-party operators, including contract start/end dates and scope of services.
3. Aggregate annual meal program revenue, expense summaries, and subsidy/utilization reports for the most recent three school years.
4. District-level reports or communications regarding meal debt, unpaid balances, or collection practices, redacted or aggregate only.
5. Descriptions and user guides for any parent/student meal account portal or online payment system currently in use.
6. Fee schedules, pricing, and administrative fees related to school meal accounts or services.
7. District policies or procedures for meal program records, data privacy, and compliance with state/federal requirements, excluding individual student data.

This request seeks public records only. It does not request private student data, individual account balances, household income records, eligibility determinations, or personally identifiable information.

Please provide records electronically where possible. If any portion requires fees, please provide an estimate in advance.

Thank you.
